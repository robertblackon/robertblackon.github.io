About

Robert Black is a registered health care practitioner who continues to pursue an interest in the study of the human body, its biomechanics, ability and potential. He has been working in the field of hands-on therapy, posture and movement for over 20 years, working in a variety of clinical settings as well as in his own practice and has built up a repertoire of skills and experience that he brings to his work. He uses Osteopathic manipulation techniques in combination with body work including massage and also exercise programs to assist you back to health. He will frequently incorporate elements from different modalities into your session depending on your requirements.
